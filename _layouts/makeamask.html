40 Second Mask

Download

You will Need

  1. Bandana
  2. Hair tyes
  3. Scissors

Step one

Fold the bandana in half.

Step Two

Fold bottom up. Fold top down.

Step Three

Place rubber bands or hair ties about 15 cm apart.

Step Four

Fold both sides to the middle and tuck.

Step Five

The mask is done. Place it over your nose and mouth.
