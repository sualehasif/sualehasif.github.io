About Us

MaskBanao, inspired by the successful campaign of masks4all.co, is a social campaign to promote the use of homemade masks in Pakistan during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Our Mission

The mission is to spread awareness about the importance of masks, push for stronger mask laws from the government, and increase mask production through homemade masks and factories. The vision is a country and community where a majority of the population is using masks, protecting each other from COVID-19. Our team includes students and professionals from around the world. To join us, write to us at contactus@maskbanao.com
